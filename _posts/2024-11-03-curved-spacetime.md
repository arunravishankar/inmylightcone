When Einstein introduced general relativity, he revolutionized our understanding of gravity. Rather than seeing it as a force, he described it as the curvature of spacetime.

## The Basics

In Newtonian physics, gravity was seen as a force between masses. However, in general relativity, massive objects curve the fabric of spacetime around them.


## Mathematical Description

The Einstein field equations describe this relationship:

$G_{\mu\nu} + \Lambda g_{\mu\nu} = \frac{8\pi G}{c^4}T_{\mu\nu}$
